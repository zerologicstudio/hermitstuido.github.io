FAQ

Common Questions

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A simple commercial might take 2-4 weeks, while complex VFX work can take 2-3 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide. We can collaborate remotely via video calls, cloud-based workflows, and have experience coordinating with international production teams.

What is your revision policy?

We include multiple revision rounds in our project quotes. Minor tweaks are always welcome during the creative process, and we work closely with you until you're completely satisfied.

Do you offer rush services?

Yes, we understand deadlines happen. Rush services are available for urgent projects, though additional fees may apply. Contact us directly for rush availability.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in whatever format you need - from web-optimized files to cinema-quality DCPs for theatrical release. We'll discuss final delivery specifications during pre-production.

How do you handle confidentiality?

We take NDAs very seriously. All our team members sign confidentiality agreements, and we have secure, encrypted systems for project file storage and transfer.
